Our Story

A Culinary Journey to the Heart of Andhra

At Aaha Bhojanam, every meal is a celebration of heritage. Originating from the rich culinary traditions of Andhra Pradesh, our kitchen brings you unadulterated, home-style cooking.

From our signature Biryanis to our comforting regional curries and thalis, we use hand-pounded spices, cold-pressed oils, and recipes passed down through generations to serve nothing but the best.

Menu Highlights

Our pride and joy—slow-cooked basmati rice layered with marinated meat, proprietary spices, and served in a traditional copper handi.
